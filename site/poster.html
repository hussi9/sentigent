Advanced

Loop Engineering — design the system that prompts the agent

A loop carries the plan to done, then learns from outcomes. Reports one honest number, FAP (Faithful Autonomous Progress).

Plan→Act→Verify→Decide→Resume
Operating layer

Harness Engineering — the machinery the loop runs inside

The guardrails wall, the clone (push-vs-ask in your voice), MCP tools, worktrees for parallel runs, and the scheduler that re-fires it.

Foundation

Context Engineering — give it what you'd otherwise type by hand

The three layers — Profile · Plan · Policy — plus project-map and the auto-written steering file. The more context, the more it can safely do alone.
